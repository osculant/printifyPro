DATA PRIVACY.

Effective Date: March 21, 2026

01. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide professional printing solutions, including:

  • Account Data: Name, business email, and billing hardware signatures.
  • Telemetry: Ink levels, nozzle health, and page counts via the Smart Panel app to facilitate Managed Print Services.
  • Print Assets: Temporary caching of print files for cloud-based processing (deleted automatically after job completion).

02. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to enhance your hardware experience. We do **not** sell user print habits or business data to third-party advertisers.

Usage includes automated ink fulfillment, preventative maintenance alerts, and improving our Heat-Free PrecisionCore™ algorithms.

03. Enterprise-Grade Security

All data transmissions between your PrintifyPro hardware and our servers are protected by **AES-256 encryption**. Industrial blueprints and medical imaging data are processed within isolated, SOC2-compliant environments.

04. Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies to maintain your session and "performance cookies" to understand how you interact with our technical documentation and driver downloads.

05. Global Compliance

PrintifyPro Global adheres to GDPR, CCPA, and international data protection standards. You may request a full export or deletion of your business data at any time via the Privacy Portal.
