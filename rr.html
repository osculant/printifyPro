RETURNS & REFUNDS.

Satisfaction Guaranteed for 30 Days

01. The 30-Day Window

We accept returns for **unopened hardware** within 30 days of delivery. If you change your mind about a purchase, you are eligible for a full refund minus a 15% restocking fee for industrial-grade units.

To initiate a return, the unit must be in its original packaging with all security seals intact.

02. Ink & Consumables

Due to the nature of chemical components, **ink bottles, cartridges, and maintenance boxes** are non-returnable once the vacuum seal is broken. If you received a defective ink unit, we will provide an immediate replacement at no cost.

03. DOA & Technical Defects

If your hardware is **Dead on Arrival (DOA)** or exhibits a mechanical failure within the first 14 days, PrintifyPro Global will cover 100% of the return shipping costs and provide a brand-new replacement unit via priority courier.
